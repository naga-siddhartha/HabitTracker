Privacy Policy — Habits

Last updated: March 2025

Habits (“the app”) is a habit-tracking app for Apple platforms. This policy describes what data the app uses and where it is stored.

1. Data the app collects and stores

All of the following are stored only on your device:

  • Habit names, descriptions, icons, and colors
  • Completion and skip history (dates and status)
  • Reminder times and settings (e.g. daily reminders)
  • Streak information (current and longest streak)
  • App preferences (e.g. appearance mode, notification on/off)

No account or login is required. The app does not collect your name, email, or any other personally identifying information.

2. How data is collected and used

Data is created and stored locally using Apple’s SwiftData framework. Reminders are delivered using the system’s local notifications only. No data is sent to our servers or any third party — the app does not make any network requests for your habit data.

3. No transmission of data

Your habit data is never sent to the internet. The app does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party SDKs that collect or transmit your data.

4. Data retention and deletion

  • Reset in the app: In Settings, you can use “Reset All Data” to delete all habits, entries, and preferences from the app.
  • Uninstalling: Removing the app from your device deletes all of its stored data.

5. Your choices and rights

You can revoke consent or request deletion at any time by using “Reset All Data” in Settings or by uninstalling the app. No data is retained by us after that, as we do not store your data on any server.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the app, use the “Contact & Support” link in the app’s Settings, or the support URL listed on the App Store page for Habits.
